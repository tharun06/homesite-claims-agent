HOMESITE INSURANCE — SAMPLE POLICY SCHEDULE
Policy Number: POL-003 | Tier: LIABILITY-ONLY (BASIC) | Effective 2026-01-01 to 2026-12-31

NAMED INSURED AND VEHICLE
This basic liability-only schedule illustrates a policy carrying no physical-
damage coverage on the insured's own vehicle. It is common for older or paid-off
vehicles. Where this schedule states a figure or omits a coverage, it overrides
the AUTO-POLICY-MASTER default.

COVERAGES AND LIMITS
- Collision: NOT covered. The insured's own collision damage is not payable.
- Comprehensive: NOT covered. Theft, fire, vandalism, and glass are not payable.
- Liability Bodily Injury: $50,000 per person / $100,000 per accident.
- Liability Property Damage: $25,000 per accident.
- Uninsured/Underinsured Motorist BI: $50,000 / $100,000.
- Medical Payments: $2,500 per person.
- Rental Reimbursement: not carried.
- Roadside: not carried.

ENDORSEMENTS ON THIS POLICY
None.

CLAIM-HANDLING IMPLICATIONS
- Damage to the insured's OWN vehicle from a collision or comprehensive peril is
  NOT payable under this policy. Confirm coverage before opening a physical-
  damage estimate.
- The policy pays for injury and property damage the insured causes to OTHERS,
  within the liability limits above.
- If the insured is hit by an uninsured driver, injuries may be covered under
  UM BI, but the insured's own vehicle damage is not (no UM property damage and
  no collision).
- No rental reimbursement is available; do not authorize a rental.
- A claim seeking own-vehicle repair on this policy should be denied for lack of
  coverage, citing the absence of collision/comprehensive on the schedule.

NOTES
This is a sample schedule for reference and training. It illustrates how a
liability-only policy limits what an adjuster can pay; always read the specific
policy on file for an actual claim.
