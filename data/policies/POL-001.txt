HOMESITE INSURANCE POLICY — POL-001
AUTO AND COLLISION COVERAGE

Section 1: Collision Coverage
This policy provides coverage for direct and accidental damage
to your insured vehicle resulting from a collision with another
vehicle or stationary object. Coverage applies regardless of
fault determination. A standard deductible of $500 applies
per claim incident.

Section 2: Comprehensive Coverage
Comprehensive coverage includes damage from non-collision events
such as theft, vandalism, falling objects, and fire. A separate
deductible of $250 applies for comprehensive claims.

Section 3: Exclusions
The following are NOT covered under this policy:
- Flood damage from external water sources
- Earthquake or natural disaster damage
- Acts of war or terrorism
- Intentional damage caused by the insured party
- Normal wear and tear or mechanical breakdown
- Racing or track use damage

Section 4: Claim Filing Procedure
Claims must be filed within 30 days of the incident.
Required documentation includes a completed claim form,
photographs of all damage, a police report if applicable,
and repair estimates from a licensed auto repair facility.
Failure to provide required documentation within 60 days
may result in claim denial.

Section 5: Rental Coverage
If your vehicle is undriveable due to a covered loss, this
policy covers rental car costs up to $50 per day for a
maximum of 30 days while repairs are completed.
