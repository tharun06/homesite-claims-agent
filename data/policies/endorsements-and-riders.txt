HOMESITE INSURANCE — ENDORSEMENTS AND RIDERS CATALOG
Document ID: ENDORSEMENTS-AND-RIDERS | Version 3.3 | Effective 2026-01-01

PURPOSE
Endorsements modify the base policy by adding, removing, or changing coverage. An
endorsement on the policy schedule always overrides the master terms for the
affected coverage. This catalog describes each optional endorsement and how it
changes claim handling.

GAP (GUARANTEED ASSET PROTECTION)
Covers the difference between the vehicle's ACV and the outstanding loan or lease
balance at total loss, up to 125% of ACV. Requires the vehicle to be financed or
leased. Excludes carryover negative equity from a prior loan and past-due
payments. On a total-loss claim with GAP, pay ACV first, then the covered
shortfall to the lienholder.

OEM PARTS ENDORSEMENT
Requires original-equipment-manufacturer parts for all repairs, overriding the
default aftermarket/recycled allowance. Estimates on OEM-endorsed policies must
specify OEM parts throughout; substitution requires insured consent.

FULL GLASS ENDORSEMENT
Waives the comprehensive deductible for glass-only claims (windshield, side, and
rear glass). ADAS recalibration remains payable as a separate line.

NEW-CAR REPLACEMENT
For vehicles in their first 24 months and under 24,000 miles, a total loss is
settled at the cost of a new comparable vehicle rather than ACV. Applies to first
total loss only.

RIDESHARE / TNC COVERAGE
Extends coverage to periods when the vehicle is logged into a transportation
network platform (e.g., during passenger pickup and transport). Without this
endorsement, losses during commercial ridesharing are excluded.

CUSTOM EQUIPMENT
Raises the custom-equipment sublimit above the standard $1,000 to a scheduled
amount for aftermarket audio, wheels, wraps, or accessibility modifications.
Requires receipts or an appraisal at binding.

ACCIDENT FORGIVENESS
A rating endorsement that prevents the first at-fault accident from increasing
premium. It does not change coverage or claim payment; it is not relevant to loss
valuation.

ROADSIDE PLUS
Upgrades standard roadside to include extended towing distance, trip-interruption
reimbursement, and a higher event count. Claim handling for roadside events
follows the roadside limits on the schedule.

RENTAL UPGRADE
Increases rental reimbursement above the standard $50/day and/or the 30-day cap to
the scheduled amount. Apply the scheduled figure in place of the master default.

HANDLING RULE
Before determining coverage on any claim, check the policy schedule for
endorsements. An endorsement changes deductibles, limits, or eligibility and must
be applied before valuation.
