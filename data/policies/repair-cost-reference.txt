HOMESITE INSURANCE — REPAIR COST AND LABOR RATE REFERENCE
Document ID: REPAIR-COST-REFERENCE | Version 5.0 | Effective 2026-01-01

PURPOSE
This reference provides standard labor rates, common part costs, and repair
operation guidelines used to validate estimates during Appraisal. Figures are
guideline benchmarks; documented regional or shop-specific variation is
acceptable.

LABOR RATE GUIDELINES (PER FLAT-RATE HOUR)
- Mechanical labor: $95 - $130
- Body labor: $55 - $75
- Frame/structural labor: $75 - $95
- Refinish (paint) labor: $55 - $70
- Diagnostic/scan time: $120 - $180 per event
Rates trend to the higher end in the Northeast and West regions and the lower end
in the Midwest and Southeast. Estimates exceeding these ranges by more than 20%
require a justification note.

PAINT AND MATERIALS
Paint materials are typically billed at a per-refinish-hour rate of $38 - $52.
Blend panels are billed at 50% of full refinish time on adjacent panels.

COMMON PART COST BENCHMARKS (OEM, BEFORE TAX)
- Front bumper cover (mainstream sedan): $350 - $650
- Headlamp assembly (halogen): $180 - $400; (LED/adaptive): $700 - $1,600
- Hood: $400 - $900
- Windshield (standard): $250 - $450; (with ADAS/camera): $900 - $1,800
- Fender (each): $180 - $420
- Airbag module (driver): $900 - $1,500; (curtain, each): $600 - $1,100

OEM VERSUS AFTERMARKET PARTS
Aftermarket or recycled parts are acceptable for non-structural components on
vehicles outside the manufacturer warranty period, subject to state rules and the
insured's endorsements. Safety-related and structural parts should be OEM unless
a certified equivalent exists. The OEM Parts endorsement requires OEM throughout.

ADAS RECALIBRATION
Any repair that removes, replaces, or disturbs a windshield, front camera, radar,
or related sensor requires ADAS recalibration. Static or dynamic recalibration is
billed at $150 - $500 per event and must appear as a separate line with
documentation.

SUPPLEMENTS
Additional damage found after teardown is handled as a supplement. Supplements
require photos of the newly discovered damage and re-approval if the revised
total crosses an authority limit or the total-loss threshold.

RENTAL DURING REPAIR
Rental is authorized at up to $50 per day for a maximum of 30 days while a covered
repair is underway, ceasing at repair completion or total-loss settlement.
