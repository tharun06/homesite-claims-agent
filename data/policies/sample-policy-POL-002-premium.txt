HOMESITE INSURANCE — SAMPLE POLICY SCHEDULE
Policy Number: POL-002 | Tier: PREMIUM | Effective 2026-01-01 to 2026-12-31

NAMED INSURED AND VEHICLE
This premium-tier schedule illustrates higher limits and added endorsements
relative to the master defaults. Where this schedule states a figure, it
overrides the AUTO-POLICY-MASTER default.

COVERAGES AND LIMITS
- Collision: covered. Deductible $250 (reduced from standard $500).
- Comprehensive: covered. Deductible $100 (reduced from standard $250).
- Liability Bodily Injury: $250,000 per person / $500,000 per accident.
- Liability Property Damage: $100,000 per accident.
- Uninsured/Underinsured Motorist BI: $250,000 / $500,000.
- Medical Payments: $10,000 per person.
- Rental Reimbursement: $75 per day, 45-day maximum (Rental Upgrade endorsement).
- Roadside: Roadside Plus.

ENDORSEMENTS ON THIS POLICY
- GAP coverage (financed vehicle).
- OEM Parts endorsement (OEM required on all repairs).
- Full Glass endorsement ($0 glass deductible).
- New-Car Replacement (vehicle is within 24 months / 24,000 miles).

CLAIM-HANDLING IMPLICATIONS
- Apply the reduced deductibles ($250 collision, $100 comprehensive) at
  settlement, not the master defaults.
- All repair estimates must specify OEM parts.
- Glass-only claims carry a $0 deductible; ADAS recalibration still applies.
- A first total loss is settled under New-Car Replacement (new comparable
  vehicle cost), not ACV.
- Rental is authorized up to $75/day for up to 45 days.
- On total loss of the financed vehicle, pay ACV or New-Car Replacement value,
  then the GAP shortfall to the lienholder.

NOTES
This is a sample schedule for reference and training. Figures illustrate how a
premium policy differs from the base terms; always read the specific policy on
file for an actual claim.
